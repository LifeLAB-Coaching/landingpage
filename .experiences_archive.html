Why book direct?

Keep it personal

Instead of fitting into a rigid platform format, guests can ask for slower pacing, more photo stops, more food focus, or a more relaxed conversational day.

Fully personalized

Booking directly allows Prateek to craft an experience that is truly tailored to you, without the constraints of standardized marketplace tours.

Perfect for private trips

These experiences work especially well for couples, friends, and small groups who want something more flexible than a standard group tour.

Meet Prateek

Kathmandu local 20+ years hosting English + Nepali

Born and raised in Kathmandu, I’m an former assistant professor, underground musician, and actor, and for more than 20 years I’ve been showing international friends around Kathmandu Valley and beyond. My background in education, music, film, and human connection shapes the way I host: relaxed, thoughtful, and personal. I speak English and Nepali, and I love helping travelers experience Nepal through local stories, conversation, food, and places that matter.
