About Prateek

Prateek Raj Neupane is a life coach, educator, musician, actor, and Kathmandu local who creates spaces for genuine human connection and meaningful personal growth. With a background in teaching leadership, psychology, design thinking, and human-centered entrepreneurship at King’s College Nepal and other organizations, he has helped people find greater clarity, confidence, and purpose. Born and raised in Kathmandu, Prateek also brings more than 20 years of sharing the valley and beyond with international friends in a way that feels personal, thoughtful, and real. Whether you meet him through coaching or curated local experiences, his approach invites you to slow down, connect more deeply with yourself and others, and experience life with greater presence, insight, and heart.
